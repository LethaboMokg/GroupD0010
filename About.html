About GroupD Med

GroupD Med is an innovative online medical booking platform founded by a group of young and passionate individuals from South Africa and Nigeria. Our founding team includes Philadelphia Sanzinza, Osam Isaac, Lethabo Mokgokoloshi, Simphiwe Hunguane, Boluwa Femi, and Prisha Pillay, who came together with a shared vision: to revolutionize the way medical appointments are booked.

Gone are the days of long waiting calls or standing in physical queues just to see a doctor. With GroupD Med, you can now effortlessly schedule appointments with the most experienced medical practitioners across South Africa and Nigeria, right from the comfort of your home or workplace. Our platform brings together a wide network of professionals to cater to your unique health needs.

We take pride in offering a user-friendly, time-saving, and efficient service that allows you to book appointments with ease. Choose from a variety of payment options, including medical aid, making healthcare accessible and convenient for everyone.

At GroupD Med, we believe that health should never be complicated. Join us and experience a seamless way to manage your healthcare journey, ensuring you always have access to the best medical care whenever you need it.
